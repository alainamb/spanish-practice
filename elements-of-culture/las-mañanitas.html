Instructions

This activity features one of many versions of Las Ma침anitas. Play the video, then work through the lyrics. Use context clues to translate the text as much as possible. Then identify all of the verbs used within in all of their forms.

Whenever you're ready, click the Reveal button to display the text with all of the verbs highlighted.

Some tips to get you started:

  • Remember that there are just three verb types: AR, ER, and IR verbs.
  • Verb use in this text is varied. The text contains both infinitive (to __) and conjugated (I __) verbs. Present, past and future tenses are covered, as are more complex grammatical structures.
  • If you enounter a word that reminds you of a verb but you don't recognize the ending, don't worry about it. You'll conjugate more and more tenses as you go! As you build up your skills, it's also good for you to notice instances of the new tenses you'll eventually be conjugating.
