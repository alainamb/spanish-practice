Elements of Mexican Culture

Introduction

The Spanish courses taught by Alaina Brandt focus in particular on Mexican Spanish, since Alaina lives and works in Mexico, and Mexico and the United States are close neighbors. Many of the classes that Alaina teaches end with a 5-10 presentation of some element of Mexican culture. These presentations are introduced and organized into categories on this page. Click on the topic to access the the full narrative and research associated with any of the element of cultures presented.

Feel free to explore these elements of culture on your own. If you’re taking a class with Alaina, we’ll decide together which element of culture to cover in each of our class meetings.

We welcome your feedback. You can contact us to share resources or to request that a specific element of culture be covered on this page.
