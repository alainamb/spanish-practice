Las vacaciones: Vacation vocabulary

Introduction

Whether you're taking a trip locally or traveling further away, knowing vacation vocabulary gives you the language to talk about where you're going, how you're getting there, where you'll stay, and what you'll do when you arrive. In Spanish, these words come up in everyday conversation when people plan ahead, swap stories, or ask each other about time off.

In this lesson, you'll review vocabulary related to travel and vacations, including transportation, accommodations, activities, and landscapes. Then you'll practice using those words to answer questions about traveling and time away.

Instructions

Review the vocabulary above and take a moment to practice pronouncing each word or phrase. Notice how the vocabulary clusters together naturally in categories like transportation words, hotel words, beach and outdoor activity words.

When you're ready, work through the questions below. Each question asks you to think about what vocabulary applies to a situation or setting. Write your answer in the text box. You don't need to write a full sentence, a list of relevant words works fine. When you click Verificar respuesta, you'll see whether your answer used relevant vocabulary and an example response.

For example:

  • Question: ¿Qué necesitas antes de salir de viaje? → Example answer: Hacer las maletas, transporte

Feel free to write full sentences if you want extra practice!
