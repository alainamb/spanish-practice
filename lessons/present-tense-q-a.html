Present Tense Verbs - Q&A Conjugation Practice

Introduction

To be able to answer questions quickly in Spanish, you need to know when to re-conjugate the verb for a new subject pronoun, and when you can keep the verb conjugation the same as it was in the question asked. This activity gives you practice identifying the correct verb form when answering questions.

Instructions

Select a category of verbs to practice. Then respond to the questions with both affirmative and negative responses.

Remember:

  • Respond in the first person to questions directed to you, whether the you is singular (tú/usted) or plural (ustedes/vosotros). You'll need to select the correct first person pronoun and conjugation from between yo and nosotros or nosotras when responding.
  • Respond in the third person (él/ella/elle/ellos/ellas/elles) for questions that are about another person or group of people.

For example:

  • Question: ¿Bailas...? → Response: Sí, bailo... or No, no bailo...
  • Question: ¿Ella baila...? → Response: Sí, ella baila... or No, ella no baila...

You're welcome to imagine how someone might fill in the question!

For example:

  • Question: ¿Bailas la salsa...?
