Expressions of Courtesy

Introduction

Welcome to the Expressions of Courtesy lesson. Here, you'll learn and practice the polite little phrases you can use to be courteous as you go about your day to day. First study examples of the types of expressions you can use to be more courteous when communicating. Then get some practice determining which expression of courtesy you can use in a variety of common social situations.

Expressions

Instructions

Familiarize yourself with the expressions of courtesy. Practice pronouncing the words aloud or in your head. Notice how the phrases go together. Imagine situations in which you'd use each expression.

When you're ready, practice expressing courtesy by responding to the scenario with an appropriate courtesy expression. Check if the courtesy expression you used is correct by clicking Show Answer.

For example:

  • Scenario: You'd like another slice of dessert. → Show Answer: Por favor or Con permiso

You're welcome to imagine longer sentence responses to each of the scenarios!

For example:

  • Que rico el postre. Más por favor (The dessert is delicious - more please)
