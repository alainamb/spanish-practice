Want to learn more about this project?

My name is Alaina Brandt, and I designed this Spanish Practice web application. I specialize in Spanish and English language learning and translation and localization. Recently, I've been developing my skills as a web designer and programmer.

I enjoy teaching Spanish very much. I consider it to be part of my lifelong language learning journey. It's rewarding to work with students and to experience their communicative growth. I understand that language learning requires ongoing practice. I also believe that taking a systematic approach can help students to internalize a language more quickly. That's why I'm developing this application.

If you'd like to learn more about my work with languages and technology, discuss collaboration opportunities, or connect about translation or natural language processing research, I'd love to hear from you.

If you're interested in studying Spanish (or a wide variety of additional languages), I encourage you to explore what UW-Milwaukee's School of Continuing Education has to offer!

Have a question about the Spanish program?

UWM's School of Continuing Education offers language classes in Spanish, Chinese Arabic and a number of additional languages, along with classes on a variety of topics, including artificial intelligence, business and health. Language courses are designed to align with the busy schedule of adult learners. Courses are offerred in the evening and on weekends, and a single course can often be completed in just a couple of months.

The program manager for the Languages program is Samantha Meyer, a leader who is incredibly supportive of her students and instructors. Samantha's background with the School of Continuing Education includes experience as a student herself. She has obtained certificates in Business Analysis and Spanish from UWM.

If you'd like to learn more about opportunities for professional development through language studies at UWM's School of Continuing Education, please feel encouraged to visit our website or reach out via email.
